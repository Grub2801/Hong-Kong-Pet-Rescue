About

Use this websites search to find pets for adoption! myPet is a website where shelters and rescues list their pets for adoption. We don’t have the pets ourselves!

Click on a link below to….

  • Find a Pet
  • Find a Shelter

Filter the list of animals for adoption based on your preferences, and you will see photos of matching pets. Click on a pet’s name to see that pet’s information. If you are interested in that pet, click on the “Ask About Me” button to contact that pet’s shelter or rescue.

You can also, search for shelters or rescue centers by name or location.
